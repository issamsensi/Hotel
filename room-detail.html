Description

The Deluxe Room at Grand Horizon Hotel is designed for discerning travelers who appreciate attention to detail. The room features a sophisticated color palette with rich textures and custom-designed furniture.

Large windows offer panoramic views of the city skyline, while blackout curtains ensure restful sleep. The work desk with ergonomic chair and ample charging ports makes it ideal for business travelers.
